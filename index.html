The underlying problem · click to expand
Single Version of Truth
Decisions made on bad information · designs built on stale data · governance documents that don't reflect what exists
⌃
The legacy assumption
Store & Retrieve — find the right repository, keep it populated, draw from a single source
→
The alternative
Observe, Reconcile & Surface on Demand — accuracy becomes a behaviour, not a location
A repository cannot keep pace with a changing estate
By the time anything is written to a CMDB or EA tool, it is already partially stale. The "single version" becomes the version of yesterday. The estate changes continuously.
Architecture truth is multi-dimensional
What is true for a solution architect designing an integration is a different slice of truth than what is true for an operations engineer responding to an incident.
Gatekeeping is the bottleneck
The "single version" model requires humans to approve updates, maintain the model, and police accuracy. This creates the bottleneck it was meant to solve.
